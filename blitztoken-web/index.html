The Problem

Independent films often cost hundreds of thousands of dollars to fund. Capital is usually provided by investors; however, investors impose restrictions on the content of films, inhibiting visionary and creative quality.

One such film is the Blitz Project - a film accompanying the artist Julius von Bismarck on his mission to create lightning sculptures and photographically immortalize. We have created a token sale platform on the Ethereum network for the Blitz Project to help fund the post-production of the film. The Blitz token framework can be used to fund future independent films.

The Film Crew

Meet the entertainment team behind the Blitz production

Viktor Jakovleski

Director

As a Germany Film and Television Academy graduate, Viktor Jakovleski has directed many award-winning films and is best known for his documentary, "Brimstone and Glory" (2017). Read more about Viktor here.

Helge Albers

Producer

Helge Albers has produced over 30 award-wining feature films and feature documentaries in the past 16 years. He is best known for his productions of “Havanna, Mi Amor” and “Above and Below". Read more about Helge here.

Martin Hanslmayr

Camera

Martin Hanslmayr has shot many award-winning films including "Still Alive". His film, "On LIMIT", was nominated for the "Best Documentary" in the European Film Award. Read more about Martin here.

Kaya Inan

Cut

Kaya is a two time nominee for "Best Editing" in the Swiss Film Awards. Some of his notable editing works include Cahier Africain (2016), Above and Below (2015) and Dalibor (2007). Read more about Kaya here.
