THE FILM CREW

Meet the entertainment team behind Blitz

Viktor Jakovleski

Director

As a Germany Film and Television Academy graduate, Viktor Jakovleski has directed many award-winning films and is best known for his documentary, "Brimstone and Glory" (2017). Read more about Viktor here.

Helge Albert

Producer

Helge Albers has produced over 30 feature films documentaries over the past 16 years. He is best known for his productions of “Havanna, Mi Amor” and “Silent Waters”. Read more about Helge here.

Martin Hanslmayr

Camera

Martin Hanslmayr has shot many award-winning films including "Still Alive". His film, "On LIMIT", was nominated for the "Best Documentary" in the European Film Award. Read more about Martin here.

Kaya Inan

Cut

Kaya is a two time nominee for "Best Editing" in the Swiss Film Awards. Some of his notable editing works include Cahier Africain (2016), Above and Below (2015) and Dalibor (2007). Read more about Kaya here.
